Lasagne

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  • Make the meat sauce.
  • Cook the noodles.
  • Make the ricotta mixture.
  • Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  • Cover with foil and bake.
  • Let the lasagna rest before serving.
